About the Model

🏗️ Architecture

EfficientNetB7 fine-tuned with a custom classification head: GlobalAveragePooling → Dense(512) → Dropout(0.5) → Dense(256) → Sigmoid output.

📁 Dataset

Trained on the IDC (Invasive Ductal Carcinoma) Histopathology Dataset with 277,524 patches from 162 whole-mount slide scans of 50×50px resolution.

⚙️ Training Strategy

Two-phase training: (1) Frozen base for head learning, (2) Top-30 layer fine-tuning with 1e-5 LR. Class weighting handles IDC imbalance.

📈 Performance

Achieves ~95–97% accuracy and ~0.98 AUC on the IDC test set. Callbacks: EarlyStopping, ReduceLROnPlateau, ModelCheckpoint on best AUC.
